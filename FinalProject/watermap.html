Overview

This visualization tool helps in the analysis and exploration of chemical components collected on soil and water samples over a thirty month period. Data used is related to different wetland pools, focusing on both water and soil characteristics.This aims to help the user gain a comprehensive understanding of the environmental conditions within the wetland areas.

Tool Guide

The home page which is the current page has a geographical map displaying information for water wetland and calculated mean for each chemical variable. A water data dropdown is located on the navigation bar which gives you the opportunity to utilize various tools.
